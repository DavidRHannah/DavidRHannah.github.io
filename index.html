About Me

I'm a passionate software developer currently pursuing my Bachelor's in Computer Science at UC Riverside, expected to graduate in Spring 2026. My journey into technology began as a curious child who loved disassembling gadgets to understand their inner workings which evolved into a career building software solutions.

I specialize in full-stack development with a strong focus on test-driven development and clean architecture. My experience spans building complex applications from interactive web platforms to system-level tools, always with an emphasis on code quality and comprehensive testing. I'm particularly drawn to projects that combine technical challenge with real-world impact.

Beyond coding, I'm driven by problem-solving and continuous learning. Whether it's optimizing performance, implementing new features, or debugging complex systems, I approach each challenge with curiosity and determination. I believe in writing code that not only works but is maintainable, testable, and scalable.
